THE RULES

KEEP IT SAFE. KEEP IT FUN. SIMPLE AS THAT.

PARK RULES

  • HELMETS REQUIRED

    ALL RIDERS UNDER 18 MUST WEAR A HELMET. STRONGLY RECOMMEND FOR EVERYONE. DON'T BE STUPID.

  • PADS ENCOURAGED

    KNEE PADS, ELBOW PADS, WRIST GUARDS. ESPECIALLY IF YOU'RE LEARNING NEW TRICKS.

  • LOOK FIRST

    CHECK YOUR LINE IS CLEAR BEFORE DROPPING. COMMUNICATE. WAIT YOUR TURN.

  • KNOW YOUR LEVEL

    DON'T HIT FEATURES YOU'RE NOT READY FOR. PROGRESS AT YOUR OWN PACE.

MORE RULES

  • NO BIKES OR SCOOTERS

    SKATEBOARDS ONLY. NO BIKES. NO SCOOTERS. KEEPS EVERYONE SAFE.

  • SKATE AT YOUR OWN RISK

    SKATEBOARDING IS DANGEROUS. YOU KNOW THE RISKS. PARENTS SUPERVISE YOUR KIDS.

  • NO GLASS

    PLASTIC OR METAL ONLY. BROKEN GLASS IS DANGEROUS FOR EVERYONE.

  • NO SUBSTANCES

    NO ALCOHOL. NO DRUGS. KEEP IT CLEAN. YOU'LL BE ASKED TO LEAVE.

  • REPORT HAZARDS

    SEE SOMETHING BROKEN OR DANGEROUS? REPORT IT IMMEDIATELY.

  • BE RESPECTFUL

    KEEP MUSIC REASONABLE. WATCH YOUR LANGUAGE. THIS IS FOR EVERYONE.

FOR PARENTS

  • WATCH YOUR KIDS

    ALWAYS SUPERVISE YOUNG CHILDREN. STAY NEARBY. WATCH FOR HAZARDS.

  • PROPER GEAR

    MAKE SURE HELMET FITS RIGHT. GET PROTECTIVE GEAR BEFORE THEY START.

  • START SLOW

    BEGINNER ZONE FIRST. LET THEM PROGRESS NATURALLY. DON'T RUSH IT.
